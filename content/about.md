# About SA1L.CC

SA1L.CC is a simple, fast QR code generator with short redirect links. Create QR codes that are easy to share and manage.

## Features

- **Quick Generation**: Create QR codes in seconds
- **Short Links**: Get memorable sa1l.cc/xxx URLs
- **Dashboard**: Manage all your QR codes in one place
- **Analytics Ready**: Track redirects with your own analytics
- **Free to Use**: No hidden costs or subscriptions

## How It Works

1. **Enter a URL**: Paste any destination URL
2. **Generate QR**: We create a unique short link and QR code
3. **Download & Share**: Get your QR code in multiple sizes
4. **Track Usage**: View all your QR codes in the dashboard

## Technology

Built with modern web technologies:

- Cloudflare Pages for hosting
- Cloudflare Workers for edge computing
- D1 for serverless database
- Firebase Auth for secure login

## Privacy

We respect your privacy:

- Only essential data is stored (URLs and user IDs)
- No tracking without your consent
- Open source approach
- Data stays in your control
