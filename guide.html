Quick Start Guide

Get started with SA1L.CC in just a few steps!

Step 1: Log In

Click the Login button and sign in with Google or GitHub.

Step 2: Enter Your URL

Paste the destination URL you want to link to. This can be:

  • A website
  • A video
  • A document
  • Any publicly accessible URL

Step 3: Generate

Click Generate QR and wait a moment while we create your unique short link and QR code.

Step 4: Download

Choose your preferred QR code size:

  • Small (256px) - for digital use
  • Medium (512px) - for print
  • Large (1024px) - for posters

Step 5: Share

Use your QR code anywhere:

  • Print on flyers or business cards
  • Share on social media
  • Include in presentations
  • Add to product packaging

Tip: All your QR codes are saved in your dashboard for easy management!
